How to install

  1. Download the zip and unzip it.
  2. Drag Nuro Optimizer.app to your /Applications folder.
  3. Launch it. On first run, grant Full Disk Access when prompted — this lets the app see system caches and launch items it would otherwise miss.
  4. Click Run a Full Diagnostic on the dashboard.
